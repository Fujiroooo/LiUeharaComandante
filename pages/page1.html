THE PERSPECTIVE OF FIGHTING GAMES

Page 1: Fighting Game Basics

One of the basic concepts of fighting games is moving. Since different games have different ways of movement, and different controllers, let's assume we are using a playstation controller. For Console games, moving the joystick is how you move. For tekken, you can use the joystick or the arrow keys. Double pressing forward or back makes you dash, double pressing up or down makes you sidestep, and normal pressing makes you walk or jump.
